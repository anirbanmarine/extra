## Form Coefficients
WPA = Waterplane area
L = Length overall
B = maximum breadth
d = maximum depth
\[C_w\] = coefficient of waterplane area
\[C_m\] = coefficient of midship area


\[ WPA = L\times B \times C_w \]

\[ Area Amidships = B \times d\times C_m \]
